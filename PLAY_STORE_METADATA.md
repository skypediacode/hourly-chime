## Full Description

A simple chime app for Phones and Watches that plays a sound at your chosen interval during the active hours you set. No ads, free to use, and free of clutter. Just choose your interval, set your active hours, select weekdays, and forget it.

Unlike an alarm app, Hourly Chime is made for ongoing interval chimes. You do not need to create a long list of alarms, and there is no snooze or stop-and-dismiss workflow. Set it once, and let it handle the timing for you.
